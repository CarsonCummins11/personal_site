Doing the CURL

06/26/2026

Climber Kyle, a local PNW trail legend, posted about a route called the CURL a few years ago, and I’ve been wanting to do it since. I got up on it on a Monday, and had a mixed bag of an experience. I started up with the fifth class south gulley route on Guye peak, which is never too much fun but not terrible.
